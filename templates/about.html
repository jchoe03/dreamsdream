MISSION
Our mission is to build 100 schools around the world for children from low-income background.
Their dreams, once seemed impossible, may come true.

SCHOOL SELECTION STANDARD
We sponsor school-building projects selected by following standards:
1. We prioritize projects involving countries ranked 100th or below in its GDP or GNP, or areas currently without a kindergarten and/or an elementary school, although we also sponsor middle and high schools.
2. We prioritize projects with construction site that has already been secured.
3. We prioritize projects proposed by those who are currently living in the corresponding country and has a prior experience in school operation and management.
4. We sponsor up to 20,000,000 KRW (about 17,000 USD) for constructing at least 2 rooms including both classrooms and offices on an already secured construction site (anything beyond school construction is not applicable to monetary funding. That is, additional classroom equipment including bookshelves, desks, etc. does not apply.)

MANAGEMENT POLICY
Fundraising status will be made known. Refer to our website and Facebook page, or attend our regular meetings for details. Following is the list of steps involved prior to initiation of and after the completion of each project.
1. Building plans, including isometric drawings, will be subject to deliberation prior to construction.
2. Process of construction will be made available to the public through our regular meetings, website, and Facebook page.
3. Report on accounts will be received and examined thoroughly.
4. School administration will be under local supervision.
